About Us

The ACM Club at North American University is a vibrant community of students passionate about computer science and technology. We aim to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning through workshops, events, and projects.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, our club provides opportunities to grow your skills, network with industry professionals, and work on exciting projects.
